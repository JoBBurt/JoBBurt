## About

I sit at the intersection of computational biology and early-stage biotech investing. My work combines PhD-level scientific rigor with venture capital execution to identify and fund transformative longevity therapeutics.

Previously, I led proteomics research at the **Buck Institute for Research on Aging**, managing multi-institution programs across 8+ organizations with $5M+ in funding. Now I translate that depth into capital deployment — building ML-powered screening frameworks that evaluate 200+ therapeutic platforms and improve deal selection by 40%.
